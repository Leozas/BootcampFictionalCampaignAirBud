Our Values

Bud sees tennis balls as one of the 7 Wonders of the World; you can gnaw on them all day, all night, forever; and they'll still hold up until they don't. Bud plans on giving everyone a tennis ball so they can play with their pets and their pets can always play with a ball even if they aren't with their owners! Pets need entertainment too!

There are many drivers that are reckless and endanger others. Bud's chased many a car that was driven by people like that. He want's to make t his job to chase every car that does that and properly enforce the law to its furthest extent; as well as feeling the wind blast by while he runs.

Bud barked up many wrong tree's in his life; but communication, discussion, and understanding can't happen if you stay away from everyone that you don't like or thinks differently. As such Bud fully supports the idea of Public Forums being revamped and rebranded. We need more dogs barking their minds!
